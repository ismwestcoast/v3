The International School of Music Westcoast provides unique group music programs for children from 2 years of age.

Piano

Nurturing a love and appreciation of music, with an emphasis on creativity and performance, the stimulating classroom environment focuses on developing music fundamentals.

Aural

ISM Westcoast's aim is to teach music as a language. The instruction is aurally based to maximise learning in young students, encouraging not only a love of music, but an understanding of music.

Reading

The concept of ISM courses is simple: what you hear, you sing; what you sing, you play; what you play, you read; what you read, you write.
